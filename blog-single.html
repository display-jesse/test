Inventory Index ~ I2

Your smart inventory system needs an even smarter health tool

"We used to get lost in the numbers and not know where to start but the inventory index, as an add-on tool, allowed us to drill down per item which enabled better decision-making. The product will highlight risk and financially alert the company on overstock/limited stock. This was a great decision."
~ Arcelor Mittal


Our external health forensics tool will enhance your current inventory management system and improve operational efficiencies. It uses actual data from ERP or inventory databases and allows you to run day-to-day operations more effectively since:

• It is a purely mathematical system that assigns your organisation a unique inventory benchmark based on historical data. This data encompasses average lead times, actual consumption time, order frequencies per line item, and worst-case scenarios.
• Set parameters are used to conduct a health check on your inventory without the need for manual input, thereby eliminating human error.
• You can view inventory results in a sophisticated and interactive report.

Pitfalls of a self-monitoring system

Most inventory management systems are end-to-end and incorporate a self-monitoring system. The fundamental problem with a self-monitoring system is that if the inventory system has a bug, factory fault, or is corrupted, the self-monitoring system could also be faulty.
In addition, most inventory management systems allow users to set inventory goals and benchmarks. As a result, the inventory benchmark is subject to prejudice and prone to human error.
Our solution systematically generates a new inventory benchmark using your historical data. The benchmark is then used to provide real-time indicators for each inventory line item, as well as alerts regarding critical inventory items that require urgent attention.

Product Functionalities

• Quick view of under-stocked and over-stocked items
• Drill-downs on each inventory item to view more detailed information
• Scoring of each inventory item, along with Rand values, based on an inventory count


This simple, but smart, tool has helped our customers to achieve enormous cash savings and improve operational efficiencies.

BI Assessment Landscape

What is a BI Landscape Assessment?

The BI Landscape Assessment is a strategy and maturity assessment implemented to determine the overall success of your organisation's BI environment. Existing BI facets of the organisation are examined and measured including your data structures, data management and technologies. Our primary goal is to ensure that your core business is supported by your BI strategy. The assessment will reveal weak points, focus areas and opportunities to improve your degree of competitiveness.

Why do I need a BI Landscape Assessment?

Far too often, an organisation’s data is poorly understood and maintained in siloed environments. It is critical that you are empowered to not only have the right BI technologies at the right time, but that you understand the intricacies of the technologies and its management in order to stay relevant to the market. Furthermore, the global pressures of today contend for a smart BI environment equipped with enhanced security.
We strive optimise your operational efficiency through a robust assessment that will provide measurable progress for the client. In addition, multiple recommendations will be provided from our leading industry experts.


What will be assessed?

The areas of analysis include, but are not limited to:

Business analysis

• Organisational Strategy
• BI and Data Strategy
• Current and Wishlist User Cases
• Pain Points and Challenges
• Stakeholder Requirements
• Business Requirements
• Data Governance and Management
• The Need for Automation

Technical analysis

• Data Sources
• Data Structures
• ETL/ELT Structures
• Data Quality and Rules
• Data Reporting
• Data Security
• Current technologies
